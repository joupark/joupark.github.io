SAIL Lab
Signal & AI Lab  ·  California Baptist University

We research at the intersection of signal processing and artificial intelligence — developing systems that hear, perceive, and act intelligently in the real world.

Research

01

Speech Enhancement

Recovering clean, intelligible speech from noisy and reverberant environments using deep neural networks and spectral masking techniques.

02

UAV Networking and Control

Autonomous flight control for unmanned aerial vehicles. Sensor fusion, state estimation, and adaptive control for stable and precise UAV operation.

03

Vision-Language-Action (VLA)

End-to-end models that ground natural language in visual perception to produce robot actions. Cross-modal alignment for embodied AI agents.

04

Multimodal Perception for V2X

Fusing visual, auditory, and linguistic signals for unified scene understanding. Includes 360° video streaming, volumetric media, and semantic-aware perception.
